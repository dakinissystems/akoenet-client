# Content moderation statement — AkoeNet

Last updated: 2026-04-06

This document describes our moderation practices as required by digital services regulations (for example, Regulation (EU) 2022/2065 — DSA), where applicable.

1. Scope
AkoeNet is a community platform (servers, channels, direct messages, voice). Users generate content; each server owner and moderator may set additional community rules.

2. Prohibited content
Users are not allowed to post or engage in prohibited content or behavior as described in our Terms, including (for guidance):
- illegal content under applicable law
- harassment, threats, serious hate speech, or discrimination
- content that infringes third-party rights (e.g., intellectual property or privacy)
- spam, malware, or fraud attempts
- abusive circumvention of blocks or moderation decisions

3. How we moderate
- Human moderation: platform administrators (when applicable) and server moderators appointed by server owners.
- Automated/technical moderation: the backend may apply filters, rejections, or rate limits for certain texts or patterns; it does not replace human review when needed.
- User reports: users can report channel messages and direct messages; reports are recorded for review.

4. Consequences
Depending on severity and recurrence, we may apply:
- deletion or editing of content
- warnings or restrictions on the account or server
- removal or bans at the server level
- suspension or termination of accounts in serious cases or when required by law

5. Appeals and complaints
If you believe a platform-level moderation decision is incorrect:
- contact the legal/DSA channel shown in the site footer (dedicated email)
- exercise data protection rights via the DPO form at `/legal/dpo`

6. Contact point for authorities and notifications (DSA)
For official communications from authorities and notifications regarding illegal content, use the legal/DSA email published on the site.

7. Changes
We may update this statement. The last review date appears at the top.

Note: This document does not constitute professional legal advice.
