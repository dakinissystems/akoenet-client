# Account deletion (AkoeNet)
Last updated: 2026-05-08

You can request account deletion in two ways:

1) From the app
In User settings, use the “Delete account / Erase account” option.

2) Through privacy (DPO)
Contact our privacy team using the DPO form available at:

`/legal/dpo`

What data is deleted or anonymized
After deletion, personal data linked to your account is anonymized or deleted, including (as guidance):
- username
- email address
- profile information (avatar, banner, bio, custom status, etc.)
- authentication credentials and sessions
- linked accounts/integrations, where applicable

What may be retained temporarily
Depending on legal obligations, platform security, and moderation needs, some records may be retained in a limited form, for example:
- moderation logs and content reports
- traces needed for abuse or fraud prevention
- information required for legal compliance

Retention periods depend on the applicable law and legitimate security needs.

Timing
- Requests made from the app are processed within the app flow; effective timing can depend on system load.
- DPO requests are handled according to the published channel in `/legal/dpo`.
